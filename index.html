Hi, there! My name is Donovan Morgan. Welcome to my portfolio! I am a Fanshawe College Graphic Design graduate who has a profound adoration for all things design related. I have experience with making t-shirt designs, poster designs, logos, instagram and other social media assets (such as posts and cover photos), as well as everything in between! I also love to draw. Though I've omitted them on this page, I frequently post new drawings and other personal projects I've done on my Instagram.
